alternations of relatively short transform fault and spreading ridge segments are represented by a boundary following the general trend 2 moving westward away from the mid-atlantic ridge the eastward-moving and more dense nazca plate is subducting under the western edge of the south american plate along the pacific coast of the continent at a rate of 77 mm 3.0 in per year 3 this collision
