refers to the indigenous religion of the germanic peoples from the iron age until christianisation during the middle ages rooted in proto-indo-european religion proto-germanic religion expanded during the migration period yielding extensions such as old norse religion among the north germanic peoples continental germanic paganism among the continental germanic peoples and anglo-saxon paganism among the old english a west germanic people among the east germanic peoples traces of gothic paganism may be discerned from scant artifacts and attestations according to john thor ewing as a religion it
